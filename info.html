History

The Sonic franchise began in 1991 when the original 'Sonic the Hedgehog', known as 'Sonic 1', was created. Since then, more than 50 sonic games and several animated shows have been created based on the character.

Purpose

This game was inspired by Sonic games and the general 2D platformer format. Players aim to defeat waves of enemies from a range of platoformer games and reach achieve maximal scores. Along the way, the gamer accumalates points which can be used to upgrade their selected character in between attempts. It also incorporates educational elements as its purpose is to interest children and amuse them while also making them use their knowledge. However, a difficulty customization was included to allow for the game be fun among older audiences as well.
